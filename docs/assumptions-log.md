# Assumptions Log

- The accessible-reader app remains a first-class sibling app rather than a temporary prototype to replace.
- The reader app will likely gain URL-based import soon, so shared storage should preserve a generic source locator instead of assuming local files only.
- The future dyslexia-friendly converter should reuse shared document variants, accessibility metadata, and reading-session models.
- The future tablet companion needs stable IDs, portable attachment references, and an append-only change history, but not full sync yet.
- The current first implementation slice is Stage 1A plus the minimum Stage 1B needed to support future Recall work safely.
